About Us

Since 1992

Universal Plastics Industries Ltd.

India's Leading Manufacturer Of PVC Flooring, Artificial Leather and PVC Films & Sheeting

An ISO: 9001-2015 Certified Company

Universal Plastics Industries Ltd. Incorporated in 1992 by Shri A.N. GOENKA, a veteran in the Industry.

Universal Plastics Industries Ltd, #1 manufacturer of Specialty Calendared Films and Sheets. Our Products include.

  • Artificial Leather for the transport industry.
  • PVC Films & Printed sheeting for the automotive, Interiors, Healthcare and general household aUPIications.
  • PVC floorings for Transport, Rail and Commercial AUPIications
  • Self Adhesive Films- Car Wrapping, Exterior and Interior Decoration Wallpapers.
  • PVC Geomembranes- Underground Structure Water Proofing
  • Swimming Pool Liners and Roofing Sheets.
